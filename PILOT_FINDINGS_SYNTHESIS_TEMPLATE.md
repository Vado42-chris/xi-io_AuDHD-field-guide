# Pilot Findings Synthesis Template

## Why this template exists

This template is meant to be used after multiple pilot sessions have been observed.

Its job is to convert individual session notes into:
- recurring patterns
- pilot blockers
- trust-language findings
- UX priorities
- product decisions

Use this after a batch of sessions, not after a single observation.
