# Pilot Post-Session Follow-Up Template

## Why this template exists

This template is meant to be sent after a pilot session or short pilot use period.

Its purpose is to:
- thank the participant
- reinforce the purpose of the pilot
- invite any late-emerging thoughts
- keep the tone appreciative without overclaiming progress

Use this as a starting point and adapt to the participant and session context.

---

# Follow-Up Template

Hi [Name],

Thank you again for taking part in the pilot.

Your feedback was genuinely useful. The goal of this stage is to learn where the product feels:
- clear or unclear
- calming or too dense
- honest or too strong in tone
- meaningful or not yet meaningful in its personalized parts

Your session helped with exactly that.

## A quick reminder

This is still a careful pilot stage, not a finished release.
So the most helpful feedback is still the most direct feedback, especially if something felt:
- confusing
- too certain
- too abstract
- not useful enough to return to

## If you notice anything after the session

Sometimes the most useful reactions happen later, after a little distance.
If anything comes to mind, even one small thought, feel free to send it.

Examples:
- what part stayed with you
- what part felt least trustworthy
- whether you would actually come back to the product
- what you would change first

Thank you again for helping shape this carefully.

[Name]
