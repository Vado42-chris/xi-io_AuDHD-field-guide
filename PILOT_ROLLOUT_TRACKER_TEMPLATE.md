# Pilot Rollout Tracker Template

## Why this template exists

This template is meant to track the operational status of a small trusted pilot.

Its purpose is to make it easy to see:
- who has been invited
- who accepted or declined
- who has been briefed
- who has been scheduled
- which batch each participant belongs to
- whether the session happened
- whether follow-up was sent
- whether the session has been synthesized into findings

Use this as a living tracker during the pilot.

---

# 1. Suggested status values

Invitation status:
- not invited
- invited
- accepted
- declined
- paused

Session status:
- not scheduled
- scheduled
- completed
- canceled
- needs reschedule

Follow-up status:
- not sent
- sent
- reply received
- no reply yet

Synthesis status:
- not added
- added to batch notes
- triaged
- decision logged

# 3. How to use this tracker

## Before outreach
Use the tracker to decide:
- how many people are being invited in the first batch
- who should stay in reserve for later batches
- who may need different session formats or timing

## During outreach
Update:
- invitation status
- whether the participant brief was sent
- whether the person accepted or declined

## Before a session
Update:
- session status
- session date
- observer / facilitator assigned

## After a session
Update:
- session status to completed
- follow-up status
- whether findings were added to synthesis
- any notable key notes or flags

## After synthesis and triage
Update:
- synthesis status
- whether the session has led to triage or decision logging

---

# 4. Suggested key notes shorthand

Use short notes like:
- high trust sensitivity
- likely async only
- follow-up likely valuable
- confusion around Learn Me
- strong personalization reactions
- pilot blocker observed
- no major friction

The detailed notes should still live in the session template.
The tracker should stay compact.

---

# 5. Honest reminder

This tracker exists to reduce pilot drift.
It should not become a substitute for real observation notes, synthesis, or triage.

Its job is operational visibility, not interpretation.
