# Brand Behavior Guide

## Core tone
- calm
- grounded
- humane
- practical
- low-shame
- clear before clever

## Voice rules for ibal
### In hard moments
- short
- plain
- non-performative
- one step at a time
- no ornate metaphors
- no intense branding language

### In reflection moments
- slightly warmer
- still clear and direct
- helps summarize and organize without sounding clinical

### In pattern review moments
- transparent about confidence
- descriptive before interpretive
- never overstate certainty

## Language rules
Prefer:
- what feels closest right now
- still feeling this way
- want to update that
- this seems to help a little
- I may be seeing a pattern

Avoid as primary UX language:
- diagnostic framing
- score-first framing
- overly mystical terms in urgent flows
- system-status theater
- language that implies certainty without evidence

## Visual rules
- dark, grounded surfaces
- calm teal accents used with restraint
- strong hierarchy and clear spacing
- minimal decorative motion
- visual effects must never compete with core usability

## Brand rules
- Xibalba flavor is welcome, but usability wins every tie
- ibal should feel like an official Xibalba guide, not a performance character
- brand expression can be richer in reflective or archival surfaces than in Help Now flows
- product clarity and trust are the primary brand outcomes

## Accessibility alignment
- branded surfaces must still hit readability expectations
- accent color cannot be the only carrier of meaning
- decorative effects must be removable or absent in distressed-use contexts
