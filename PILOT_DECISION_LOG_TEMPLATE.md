# Pilot Decision Log Template

## Why this template exists

This template is meant to capture explicit product decisions made because of pilot findings.

Pilot work often produces lots of observations, but the real risk is that decisions remain implicit.
A good decision log makes it clear:
- what was decided
- why it was decided
- what evidence supported it
- what the team will do next
- what risks were accepted, if any

Use one copy of this template per decision.
