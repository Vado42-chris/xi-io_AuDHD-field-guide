# Trusted Pilot Invitation Template

## Why this template exists

This template is meant to be adapted when inviting someone into the small trusted pilot.

It should help keep outreach:
- clear
- honest
- non-overclaiming
- respectful of the pilot's limited scope

Use this as a starting point, not as rigid final copy.

---

# Invitation Template

Hi [Name],

I’m inviting a very small number of trusted people to try an early pilot version of a support tool I’ve been building.

The product is designed to help a person:
- identify what state they are in
- try a calmer first action
- log whether it helped
- slowly build more personalized patterns over time

This is **not** a public launch and it is **not** being presented as a finished or fully validated product.
It is still in a careful refinement stage.

The reason I’m reaching out is that I want grounded feedback on questions like:
- is the main flow understandable?
- does it feel calming or too dense?
- does the trust language feel honest and clear?
- do the personalized parts feel meaningful?

## What participation would involve

If you join, I may ask you to:
- try a guided session or a short self-directed session
- react to the main support flow
- share where the product feels useful, confusing, too certain, or too vague
- optionally give follow-up feedback after using it more than once

## Important caveats

This product is not:
- a medical device
- a diagnosis tool
- a crisis authority
- a finished system

The pilot is specifically meant to learn where the product is working and where it still needs improvement.
